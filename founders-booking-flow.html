❓ Question 1 How should we send the confirmation?

OptionWhat the buyer gets in their inboxProsCons
A — Bundled (current) 1 email: "4 seats for Kombucha on 12 May" + 1 .ics + 1 invoice Cleanest inbox Hard to forward just one ticket. HR can't hand out individual tickets.
B — Separate emails N emails (one per seat), each with its own ticket + .ics, all to the buyer Buyer can forward each one to a friend / colleague Inbox flooded — 8 emails for one purchase feels noisy
C — Bundled + printable PDF tickets 1 email + N individual ticket PDFs attached + 1 invoice Best of both: clean inbox, easy to forward AND print. Works equally well for friends and HR teams. Takes us a bit longer to build
👉 My recommendation: C — it's the only option that also covers your B2B case cleanly.
